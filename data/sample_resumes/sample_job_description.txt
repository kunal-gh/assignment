Job Title: Senior Machine Learning Engineer — NLP & Resume Intelligence

Location: Remote / Hybrid — San Francisco Bay Area preferred

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABOUT THE ROLE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

We are building an AI-powered hiring platform that makes recruitment smarter,
faster, and fairer. As a Senior ML Engineer, you will design and ship the core
NLP pipeline that powers our resume screening and candidate ranking engine.

You will work across the full ML lifecycle — data collection, modelling,
evaluation, deployment, and monitoring — with a strong focus on semantic
understanding and fairness-aware AI.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Programming & Foundations:
  - Python (5+ years, production-grade code)
  - Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and complexity
  - SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis)
  - Git and collaborative development workflows

Machine Learning & NLP:
  - PyTorch or TensorFlow (production model training and serving)
  - Natural Language Processing: spaCy, NLTK, Hugging Face Transformers
  - Sentence embeddings and semantic similarity (sentence-transformers, BERT)
  - Vector databases and similarity search: FAISS, Pinecone, or Qdrant
  - Resume parsing: experience with PDF/DOCX text extraction (PyMuPDF, pdfplumber)
  - Ranking systems: familiarity with NDCG, MAP, Precision@K

Systems & Infrastructure:
  - REST API development: FastAPI or Flask
  - Docker and container orchestration (Kubernetes preferred)
  - Cloud platforms: AWS (SageMaker, S3, EKS) or GCP (Vertex AI)
  - CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, CircleCI, or similar)

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PREFERRED / NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  - Experience with fairness-aware ML and bias detection (Fairlearn, AIF360)
  - Understanding of EEOC guidelines and four-fifths rule for adverse impact
  - Streamlit or similar dashboard/visualisation experience
  - OpenAI API integration and prompt engineering
  - MLflow, Weights & Biases, or similar experiment tracking tools
  - Experience with multimodal data (structured + unstructured text)
  - Published research or open source contributions in NLP or IR

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT YOU WILL DO
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  - Design and own the resume understanding pipeline (parsing → embedding → ranking)
  - Research and evaluate embedding models for semantic resume-JD matching
  - Build and maintain fairness monitoring systems to ensure EEOC compliance
  - Develop explainable AI components that justify candidate rankings in plain English
  - Collaborate with Engineering on API design and performance optimisation
  - Write thorough tests, monitoring dashboards, and technical documentation
  - Mentor junior ML engineers on NLP best practices and system design

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IDEAL CANDIDATE PROFILE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  - 5–8 years of hands-on ML/NLP engineering experience
  - At least 2 years deploying models to production at meaningful scale
  - Comfortable owning a problem from research to production
  - Strong communicator who can translate ML results into business decisions
  - Passionate about using AI responsibly, with awareness of bias and fairness
